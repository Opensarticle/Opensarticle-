Who We Are

Oniarticle is an open-access scientific portal designed to bridge the gap between abstract theoretical frameworks and the global research community. Founded on the principles of intellectual freedom and mathematical rigor, we provide a robust platform for authors to publish high-quality manuscripts and conceptual articles without the barriers of traditional academic gatekeeping.

Our platform represents a shift in scientific dissemination. We believe that groundbreaking insights often emerge from independent researchers who possess the focus to explore unconventional models of reality.

Our Core Mission

At Oniarticle, our mission is to ensure that every revolutionary concept--from field-based manifestation theories to advanced mechanical frameworks--has a permanent, high-authority digital record. We aim to build a global library of knowledge that remains accessible to all seekers of truth.

Open Access

We believe in the free flow of information. All peer-submitted research is accessible to the public at no cost.

Technical Authority

By enforcing strict content standards, we ensure that every paper serves as a high-relevance source for the scientific community.

Global Visibility

Our advanced SEO architecture ensures that independent research receives the global reach it deserves.

The Vision of the Founder

Founded by Anshik Pathak, a theoretical researcher dedicated to field-based mechanics, Oniarticle reflects a commitment to the transparency of knowledge. The platform serves as a legacy for the next generation of thinkers who prioritize "First Principles" over institutional dogma.
