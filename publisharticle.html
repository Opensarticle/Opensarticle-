General Submission Guidelines

Welcome to the Oniarticle submission portal. We are an open-access platform designed for scientists, engineers, and independent researchers to showcase their work to the world. Our primary objective is to maintain a high standard of digital literature while ensuring maximum discoverability through search engine optimization.

  • Word Limit: For optimal SEO performance, we recommend articles between 1000 and 2000 words. Submissions under 1000 words may be flagged as "thin content" and might face delayed indexing.
  • Authorship: Please provide your full legal name as it should appear on the published page. Anonymous submissions are currently not accepted.
  • Integrity Labels: It is mandatory to disclose if your article was written by a human or assisted by AI. This helps us apply the correct "Metadata Label" for search engine transparency.
  • Formatting: Ensure your text is organized into clear paragraphs. Use of sub-headings is encouraged to improve readability and user engagement scores.
  • Review Process: All submissions undergo a manual verification by the Oniarticle admin panel. Approved articles usually go live within 24 to 48 hours.

By publishing on Oniarticle, you join a growing ecosystem of modern thinkers. Whether you are submitting a paper on Aerospace Engineering, Digital Marketing, or Theoretical Manifestation, our platform ensures your voice is heard without the hurdles of complex peer-review paywalls. We believe that knowledge should be free, accessible, and highly optimized for the digital age.

Note: Once you click submit, you will be redirected to WhatsApp to share your final draft. Our admin will then verify the content for quality and SEO standards before the final upload to the Oniarticle server.
